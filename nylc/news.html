Ogun Elegant Youth League News

League Updates

November 3, 2024 - The Ogun Elegant Youth League has announced the dates for the upcoming season starting in January 2025. The league will feature 20 teams from across the state.

Match Highlights

October 30, 2024 - The spectacular match between Abeokuta Warriors and Ijebu United ended in a thrilling 2-2 draw, showcasing some incredible young talent. Highlights included two stunning goals from both teams in the last 10 minutes.

Player Spotlights

October 25, 2024 - This week's spotlight is on Adebayo Adewale, a forward from Sagamu Strikers, who has scored 10 goals in the last five matches, setting a new league record.

Upcoming Matches

November 10, 2024 - Don't miss the upcoming match between Ota Eagles and Ewekoro Giants, a highly anticipated game that promises to be full of excitement.

Events

Join us for the Annual Youth Soccer Gala on December 15, 2024, where we will celebrate the achievements of our young athletes and their contributions to the sport.

Teams

Our league features teams from across Ogun State, including Abeokuta Warriors, Ijebu United, Sagamu Strikers, and many more.
